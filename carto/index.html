Why Data Teams Switch from CARTO to Dekart

"We dropped CARTO because of the price"

CARTO does not publish pricing. Teams we talk to report contracts starting around $4,000+/month. Dekart starts free, with paid plans from $49/month. No sales calls, no negotiations.
👉 See Dekart Cloud pricing

"CARTO copies my data instead of querying my warehouse"

CARTO requires data ingestion into their platform. Dekart queries BigQuery and Snowflake directly. Your data never leaves your warehouse. No ETL pipelines, no sync jobs, no extra storage costs.

"I just want to write SQL, not learn a builder UI"

CARTO's Builder interface adds complexity on top of what data teams already know: SQL. Dekart is SQL-first. Write a query, see a map. No drag-and-drop layers, no widget configurations. If you know SQL, you already know Dekart.

"We need to self-host for compliance"

CARTO offers self-hosting only on enterprise plans. Dekart runs as a single Docker container on any infrastructure, including the free open-source version. Deploy on GKE, App Engine, or any Docker host. Your data, your network, your rules.
👉 Self-hosting options

"We got locked into CARTO and can't leave"

Dekart is fully open-source (AGPL). Your maps are SQL queries stored in your warehouse. There is nothing proprietary to migrate away from. Switch to self-hosted, switch to cloud, or fork the code. You always own your work.
